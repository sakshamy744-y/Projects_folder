About

Engineering meets craft.

The story, the stack, and the standards behind the work.

SY 📍 Lucknow, India

I'm Saksham — a full-stack developer who treats the web as a craft, not a checklist. Over the past several years I've partnered with founders, agencies, and small teams to turn rough ideas into interfaces people actually enjoy using.

My approach blends solid engineering fundamentals with an eye for motion and detail: things should not just work, they should feel considered. Whether that's a hero moment or a form that validates gracefully, the standard stays the same.

When I'm not shipping client work, I'm exploring new front-end techniques, contributing to small open-source tools, and refining this very site as a live sandbox.
